Our mission

At Skidemy, we believe that education should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their location or background. That's why we've created an online learning platform that offers high-quality courses taught by experts in their fields. Our mission is to provide affordable, flexible, and engaging learning experiences to individuals around the globe.

Our platform is designed to help learners acquire new skills and knowledge, advance their careers, or pursue their passions. Whether you're looking to learn a new language, enhance your technical skills, or simply explore a new topic, Skidemy has the course for you.

We understand that everyone learns differently, which is why we offer a variety of courses in different formats, such as live online classes, pre-recorded videos, and interactive tutorials. This allows learners to choose the learning style that works best for them.

Our team of dedicated instructors is passionate about their subjects and committed to helping learners achieve their goals. We also offer personalized support to help learners stay on track and overcome any challenges they may face.

At Skidemy, we believe that education is the key to unlocking a better future. Join us today and take the first step towards achieving your goals!

Who are we?

At Skidemy, our team is made up of passionate professionals who are dedicated to providing the best possible online learning experience to our students. Our team includes Samuel, our programmer, Andreu, our cyber security expert, and Alan, our CEO.

Meet Samuel, our programmer. With over a decade of experience in software development, Samuel is responsible for building and maintaining the Skidemy platform. He's passionate about creating innovative solutions that make learning accessible and enjoyable for all.

Next up is Andreu, our cyber security expert. Andreu has spent the last 10 years working in the field of cyber security, helping companies protect their digital assets from cyber attacks. At Skidemy, he's responsible for ensuring that our platform is safe and secure for our students, so they can focus on learning without worrying about their data.

Finally, we have Alan, our CEO. Alan has over 20 years of experience in the education industry, and he's passionate about using technology to make learning more accessible and engaging. He's responsible for setting the overall direction of Skidemy, and he works closely with the rest of the team to ensure that we're delivering on our mission to provide high-quality, affordable education to everyone.

Together, Samuel, Andreu, and Alan make up the core of the Skidemy team. With their combined expertise and passion for education, they're committed to building a platform that empowers learners around the world to achieve their goals.
